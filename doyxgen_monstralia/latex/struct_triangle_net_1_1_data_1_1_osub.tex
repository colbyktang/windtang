\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
An oriented subsegment. 

Iincludes a pointer to a subsegment and an orientation. The orientation denotes a side of the edge. Hence, there are two possible orientations. By convention, the edge is always directed so that the \char`\"{}side\char`\"{} denoted is the right side of the edge.
