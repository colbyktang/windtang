For example, if the ground was found at y=0, collision\+Offset = 2 type = Capsule and height = 3 then the physics system will be queried to see if there are any colliders in a capsule for which the bottom sphere that is made up of is centered at y=2 and the top sphere has its center at y=2+3=5.

{\footnotesize\ttfamily float Pathfinding.\+Graph\+Collision.\+diameter = 1F}



Diameter of capsule or sphere when checking for collision. When checking for collisions the system will check if any colliders overlap a specific shape at the node\textquotesingle{}s position. The shape is determined by the \mbox{\hyperlink{class_pathfinding_1_1_graph_collision_a99ee22c124f171badfb22a33a8372007}{type}} field. 

A diameter of 1 means that the shape has a diameter equal to the node\textquotesingle{}s width, or in other words it is equal to \mbox{\hyperlink{class_pathfinding_1_1_grid_graph_a8a90724b91f172aa906a0b14d69b3f61}{node\+Size }}.
