Calculate the shortest path through the funnel. 

If the unwrap option is disabled the funnel will simply be projected onto the XZ plane. If the unwrap option is enabled then the funnel may be oriented arbitrarily and may have twists and bends. This makes it possible to support the funnel algorithm in XY space as well as in more complicated cases, such as on curved worlds. \mbox{[}Open online documentation to see images\mbox{]}
