\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The triangle data structure. 

Each triangle contains three pointers to adjoining triangles, plus three pointers to vertices, plus three pointers to subsegments (declared below; these pointers are usually \textquotesingle{}dummysub\textquotesingle{}). It may or may not also contain user-\/defined attributes and/or a floating-\/point \char`\"{}area constraint\char`\"{}.
